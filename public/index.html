Our Mission

We help you understand, manage, and defend against the full spectrum of online risks. From foundational security practices to handling emerging threats, we provide the straightforward guidance you need to stay safe in every digital situation.

How We Can Help You

Password Security

We'll assist you in setting up strong passwords using a password manager and guide you on implementing MFA for added security.

Open Source Intelligence (OSINT)

Discover what information about you is public online and learn how to manage and protect your digital footprint.

Data Recovery

Need to recover data from an old device? We can help retrieve files from desktops, laptops, phones, or other storage devices.

Threat Management

Our team will investigate your network to uncover malicious activity and highlight vulnerable areas (Threat Hunting). We'll also help identify and remove malicious software that may be lurking on your devices without your knowledge (Threat Removal).

On-Demand Tech Assistance

Get the help you need, right when you need it. This service covers everything from password resets and email configuration to resolving connectivity issues and providing desktop support. Consider us your always-available, friendly IT experts, ready to jump in and solve any technical hurdle that slows you down.

Vulnerability Scanning & Cleanup

We go beyond basic protection to secure your digital environment. This includes comprehensive vulnerability scanning to pinpoint system weaknesses, coupled with the use of advanced tools to find and eliminate stubborn, persistent malware that standard software often fails to detect.

Basic Penetration Testing

Our basic penetration testing service helps you identify and understand your digital footprint and discover potential vulnerabilities. We'll simulate a basic attack to highlight exposed information and insecure access points before a malicious actor does.

  • Website Footprint Analysis: Discover what public information is available about your website or online presence.
  • Exposed Access Point Identification: We'll scan for common vulnerabilities and open ports on your network.
  • Summary Report: Receive a clear, actionable report detailing our findings and recommendations for improvement.
